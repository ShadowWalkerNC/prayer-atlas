# Prayer Atlas

Prayer Atlas is a browser-based prayer and missions platform that helps people discover prayer needs by location through an interactive map experience.

Users can explore a country, state, or town and see missionaries, ministries, and active prayer requests connected to that area.

## Purpose

The goal of Prayer Atlas is to make prayer more visible, relational, and organized. Instead of reading scattered updates or static lists, users move through a map, open a place, and immediately understand who is serving there, what needs prayer, and how they can respond.

## How it works

A user opens the application and lands on a map-based home screen. From there, the user can search for or click a location, open a region view, and browse the people, organizations, and prayer requests associated with that region.

Each region can include:

- Missionary or ministry profiles
- Prayer requests with urgency, category, and date
- Updates that show progress or answered prayer over time
- Engagement actions such as "I prayed," save, follow, or encouragement messages

The user journey is intentionally simple:

1. Open the map.
2. Choose a country, state, or city.
3. Read the local prayer needs.
4. Respond by praying, saving, or following updates.
5. Return later to see what changed.

## Main users

Prayer Atlas is designed for several types of users:

- Individual believers who want a simple way to pray for people in specific places
- Churches that want a modern missions and prayer wall experience for their members
- Missionaries and ministries that need a structured place to share requests and updates
- Administrators who review submissions and protect sensitive information before publication

## Core features

The initial application should focus on a practical MVP rather than a full mission-management platform. The first release should include:

- Interactive map with region drill-down
- Region pages or drawers that summarize local prayer activity
- Public prayer request feed by location
- Basic profile cards for missionaries or ministries
- "I prayed" and saved/follow actions
- Admin review and moderation tools
- Support for answered prayer and update posts

## Why the app matters

Prayer Atlas turns a broad idea like "pray for the world" into something concrete and usable. The map gives people a sense of place, the profiles make the people real, and the request flow helps users participate in a repeated rhythm of prayer instead of a one-time interaction.

For organizations, the app also creates a clearer system for collecting, organizing, and sharing requests without relying on scattered emails, PDFs, or hallway bulletin boards.

## Product summary

Prayer Atlas is a map-based prayer platform where users discover missionaries, ministries, and prayer requests by location, then respond through prayer, follow ongoing updates, and stay connected to what is happening in that region.
